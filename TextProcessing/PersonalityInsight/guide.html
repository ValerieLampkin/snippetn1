About

Understand which documents are related or extract high-level concepts mentioned in text.Concept Insights offers a set of services that enable you to do content recommendation and exploration. For instance, using Concept Insights you can provide a document, understand the high level concepts mentioned in it and recommend related documents. In another example, you can go beyond simple keyword searches to discover content that mentions concepts that are relevant to a query.
